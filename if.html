Intellect Forge



Date and Time

March 21, 2024 (Thursday) & 10:00 AM Onwards





Location

C-Block, Third Floor, Room No. 03 & 19 (CT-03 & CT-19), Manav Rachna International Institute of Research & Studies, Sector – 43, Aravalli Hills, Delhi - Surajkund Road, Faridabad – 121004, (Haryana), India


Faculty Coordinator

Dr. Sonia Duggal

Dr. Priyanka Dadhich


Screening Phase

1. For all the entries the screening test will be conducted.
2. The students will participate in teams. 30 questions will be asked within a time limit of 30 minutes.
3. Questions related to mental ability, logical reasoning, and verbal and non-verbal reasoning will be asked for screening.
4. If two or more teams tie, then the next round of screening will be conducted, and for these, 15 questions within the time limit of 15 min. will be given to teams.
5. Five teams will be selected for the final Stage Round.
6. In the stage round, a total of 4 rounds will be there. For the first three rounds, four questions will be asked to each team based on General Awareness, Mythology, General Knowledge, Sports, Movies, etc. One round will be based on audio–visual clips related to punch lines, company logos, famous personalities, and songs from movies.
7. Round 4 will be a rapid-fire round. In this, a total of 15 questions will be asked to each team within one minute.

Round 1 (Rules)

1. In this round four questions will be asked to each team.
2. Four options will be given to each question.
3. This is a non-transferrable round. The time limit is 20 seconds.
4. 10 Marks will be given for each correct answer.
5. Maximum marks that can be scored in this round is 40.
6. There will be NO negative marking.

Round 2 (Rules)

1. In this round four questions will be asked to each team.
2. No options will be given.
3. The time limit is 20 seconds.
4. 10 Marks will be given for each correct answer.
5. This is a transferable round. Bonus marks +5 will be given to the next team.
6. There will be Negative marking in this round.
7. 5 marks will be deducted for the wrong answer.

Round 3 (Rules)

1. In this round four questions will be asked to each team.
2. Maximum Marks scored in this round by each team is 40.
3. This is a non-transferrable round.
4. The time limit is 20 seconds.
5. 10 Marks will be given for each correct answer.
6. There is No negative marking in this round.
