ABOUT MANAV RACHNA INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF RESEARCH & STUDIES (MRIIRS)

Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS, formerly MRIU- a NAAC Accredited ‘A++’ Grade Institution), ‘Deemed-to be-University’ under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956, offers programs from Graduate to Doctoral level that are in tune with the market dynamics. The Institution has been conferred with 5-Star rating for Teaching, Employability, Academic Development, Facilities, Social Responsibility and Inclusiveness by QS Rating System. Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies has been rated in the prestigious NIRF India Rankings 2022: Engineering – Rank 105, University – Rank Band 101-150.

ABOUT SCHOOL OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS (SCA)

School of Computer Applications was established with a focus on imparting the fundamental concepts both theoretically and practically. Keeping in view, the growth in industry and increasing demand for computer professionals, the department offers some industry associated courses such as MCA, BCA with various specializations in association with Microsoft, B.Sc.(Information Technology) with Dual Specialization and Ph.D. programmes. To meet the needs of industry and R&D organizations, the course curriculum has been carefully customized to meet the rising global outlook and focuses on forthcoming innovations in the field of Computer Applications.
